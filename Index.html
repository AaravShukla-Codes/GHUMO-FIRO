✈️ Smart Travel Tips

📄 Essential Documents

Always carry valid ID proof, passport (for international travel), tickets, and hotel bookings. Keep both digital and physical copies. Store backup copies in your email or cloud storage.

💰 Budget Planning

Plan your daily expenses including food, transport, and activities. Keep extra cash for emergencies and use digital payments when possible. Track spending to avoid overspending.

🎒 Smart Packing

Pack light and carry only essentials. Use packing cubes for organization. Always include basic medicines, chargers, and weather-appropriate clothing.

📱 Stay Connected

Get a local SIM card or international roaming plan. Save offline maps and important contacts. Keep your phone charged with a power bank.

🛡️ Safety First

Avoid unsafe areas, especially at night. Keep your valuables secure and be aware of your surroundings. Share your itinerary with a trusted person.

🍜 Try Local Food

Explore local cuisines but eat from clean and hygienic places. Stay hydrated and avoid unsafe water. Carry snacks for long journeys.
