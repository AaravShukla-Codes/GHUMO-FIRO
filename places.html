🌍 Must-Visit Places Around the World

Explore unique destinations known for culture, nature, and unforgettable experiences.

🏜️ Jaipur, India

Known as the Pink City, Jaipur is famous for its royal palaces, forts, and rich heritage.

Famous For: Hawa Mahal, Amber Fort

Best Time: October to March

🏔️ Leh-Ladakh, India

A paradise for adventure lovers with high mountains, monasteries, and breathtaking roads.

Famous For: Bike trips, Pangong Lake

Best Time: May to September

🌉 Sydney, Australia

A vibrant city known for its harbour, beaches, and iconic architecture.

Famous For: Sydney Opera House, Harbour Bridge

Best Time: September to November

🏝️ Maldives

Luxury island destination with crystal clear water, private villas, and coral reefs.

Famous For: Water villas, scuba diving

Best Time: November to April

🏞️ Switzerland

Known for snow-covered Alps, scenic train journeys, and beautiful lakes.

Famous For: Mountains, skiing, chocolates

Best Time: December to March (snow), June to September (green valleys)

🏜️ Cairo, Egypt

One of the oldest cities in the world, home to ancient pyramids and rich history.

Famous For: Pyramids of Giza, Sphinx

Best Time: October to April
